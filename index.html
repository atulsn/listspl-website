C-KYC Solutions

Seamless Central KYC registration and compliance for banks.

View Details▼

CKYC is a web-based application used to upload the KYC details of bank customers to CESAI to get a Centralized KYC ID. It is also used to get KYC details of new customers by CKYC ID. Central KYC Registry is a centralized repository of KYC records with uniform norms — reducing the burden of producing KYC documents every time a customer creates a new relationship with a financial entity.

Highlights of C-KYC
Helps to provide bank customer's centralized KYC information.
Allow customers to access CKYC details from other banks also.
No need to re-enter existing customer's information while making new KYC registration.
Facility to map downloaded data from another software.
Facility to view different CKYC reports.
Facility to conversion of bulk amount of customers at a time in CESAI acceptable format.
🛡️

Anti-Money Laundering

Robust AML software to monitor and flag suspicious activity.

View Details▼

LIST AML is a web-based application to identify and control money laundering and financial terrorism activities. AML gives a view of approximate or exact matching of banned entities with bank customers (Name Screening). Customer Risk management is also built in. The system generates statutory reports for Financial Intelligence Unit-India (FIU-IND) in its prescribed format. AML is tightly integrated with our CBS and can be integrated with any other CBS deployed in the organization.

Highlights of AML
AML System helps banks/financial institutes to trace out illegal activities like money laundering, financing terrorism and report it to FIU-IND with automated process.
Effective management to control money laundering and terrorism financing.
AML Facility keeps watch on customers to verify whether they exist in the banned list or not.
Automated alert generation engine which runs periodically at set frequency and generates alerts.

CTS Interface

Cheque Truncation System for paperless cheque clearance.

View Details▼

Cheque truncation means stopping the flow of physical cheques issued by a drawer to the drawee branch. An electronic image of the cheque is sent to the drawee branch along with MICR fields, date of presentation, presenting bank etc. This eliminates the need to move physical instruments across branches — reducing time, cost of transit, and delays in processing.

Highlights of CTS Interface
CTS interface eliminates the time, money and manpower wasted during physical movement of cheques from banks to clearing house.
Enables faster clearing and better service to customers.
Simple and user-friendly graphical user interface.
Bitonal Front & Back, Grey scale images.
Automatic encoding and endorsing.
Data file encryption before transmission to service branch/Clearing House.
Enables user to avoid duplicate MICR cheques.

Audit Module

Comprehensive audit tracking and reporting for banks.

View Details▼

LIST audit module is specially made for audits and audit-related work. Every single audit point can be tracked and the software will display if the same audit point is getting repeated. The software follows all audit standards like Concurrent Audit, Internal Audit, Statutory Audit etc. This web-based module conducts the audit process in a systematic and automated format — maintaining batch-wise audit records through stages like Delegation, Compliance, Verification and Confirmation.

Highlights of Audit Module
LIST audit module gives facility to define static and dynamic heads.
This module is used to report abnormalities in functioning / transactions in branches.

Credit Appraisal System

Smart system to analyze credit worthiness of borrowers.

View Details▼

LIST credit appraisal system is a smart banking system to analyze credit worthiness of borrowers. It helps in getting decisions while disbursing loans, calculates risk ratios according to the borrower's financial behavior, and helps to discover the risks associated with the extension of the credit facility. Financial risk is assessed in terms of static ratios — measuring the performance and financial indicators of the borrower.

Highlights of CAS
Low risk arises in credit.
Comprehensive web-based data management system with web-based Reporting Engine and easy to use menu-driven application.
Supports tracking of exposures at various levels to minimize risk.
Dynamic hierarchy creation.

ABPS / DBTL / NACH

Centralized electronic transfer for direct benefit disbursement.

View Details▼

A centralized electronic transfer system to get direct mandates from correspondent credit banks for disbursing allowance using Aadhaar number. DBTL scheme enables LPG customers to get subsidy directly to their bank accounts. Arista Payment Solutions generates mapper files and after approving them, it updates to CBS also. DBTL also needs to approve mandates and requires additional information regarding LPG connection.

Highlights of ABPS/DBTL
It excludes unnecessary delays and paper work involved in the existing process.
Arista Payment Solutions makes simplification of the mandate acceptance and recording process.
Process of transferring subsidies and benefits into Aadhaar-mapped bank accounts will become seamless.
This application enables smooth conversion of downloaded file and generation of return file to be uploaded.
Banks can provide faster service to their customers.

Agency Banking

Doorstep banking services through engaged agents.

View Details▼

Agency Banking provides limited scale banking and financial services to members through engaged agents under a valid agency agreement. It is designed for agents — agents can create new customers and do different types of transactions. Doorstep Banking provides banking services to members through employees of the Bank or Engaged Agents, offering an economical way to serve customers in remote areas rather than opening a branch.

Highlights of Agency Banking
The customer will get the services at their doorstep by the employee of the Bank or by an appointed agent of the Bank.
New customers from remote locations will be added to the Bank's Customer Base who may otherwise remain away from Banking.
Customers who are physically challenged or unable to walk into the branch will get services for Deposit/Withdrawal of Cash.

Loan Document Printing

Automated loan document generation integrated with CBS.

Loan Document Printing is a web-based application developed to generate and print documents required in and after the loan sanctioning process. The system is tightly integrated with CBS. This application standardizes legal document formats drafted in consultation with legal consultants — avoiding manual mistakes and controlling unwanted alterations. The system has the facility to print one or more documents at a time.

Helps to avoid unwanted modifications which may occur by mistake.

Statement on email module is a web-based application for facilitating email service for account statements. The system is integrated with CBS and provides facility of scheduling emails for statements — including the feature of designing own email templates. e-Passbook

Electronic passbook — online and offline access 24x7.

View Details▼

LIST e-Passbook is a mobile application providing an electronic passbook to bank customers — available both online and offline. Once registration is done, internet connectivity is only required to sync with CBS for updated data. The application provides electronic passbook through mobile phones, account details, account statements, bank holidays list, and a personal ledger. Available in two modes — offline and online — with different MPINs for each.

Highlights of e-Passbook
It reduces Passbook printing and stationary cost.
Online and offline access 24x7.
Faster service can be provided to bank customers.
Search transaction history based on date, amount and transaction description.
Statements can be obtained on mail in PDF/XLS format.
Personal Ledger can be created.
